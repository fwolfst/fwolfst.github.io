symbols for pads

2020-11-02

While using “Pads” (like etherpad lite, cryptpad etc.) or other poor text editors you sometimes might need symbols to indicate lists, important items, separate sections and other stuff. This is the beginning of a collection of mine.

Copy and paste them as you like

For technical reference: these are UTF-8 symbols and will probably not be supported by real legacy software.
